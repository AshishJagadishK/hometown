About Kannur

Kannur, also known as Cannanore, is a city in the state of Kerala, India. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, pristine beaches, and historical landmarks.

The city has a long history dating back to ancient times and has been an important center of trade and commerce. Kannur was a major trading port for spices, timber, and other commodities, attracting traders from across the world.

Today, Kannur is a vibrant city known for its traditional art forms, festivals, and natural beauty. It is a popular tourist destination with attractions such as beaches, forts, and wildlife sanctuaries.
